1. Ordering a Specific Type of Cuisine:
       Zomato.<country>.cuisine.<cuisine_name> (e.g., Zomato.India.cuisine.Italian)
	   
2. Ordering from a Specific Type of Restaurants:
       Zomato.<country>.restaurants.<restaurant_type> (e.g., Zomato.India.restaurants.Cafe)
	   
3. Ordering from a Specific Zip Code/Pincode:
       Zomato.<country>.zipcode.<zipcode> (e.g., Zomato.India.zipcode.400001)
